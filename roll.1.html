NAME

roll

SYSNOPSIS

roll DICE_ROLL_EXPR [DICE_ROLL_EXPR …]

DESCRIPTION

roll parses the dice roll expressions and models the rolling of the dice described by the expressions. Dice roll expressions are forms by an integer (number of dice) the letter “d” followed by an integer (number of sides) and optionally follow by a plus or minus and integer which is treated as an offset.

roll is part of the gameset collection of game programs.

EXAMPLE

Rolling one twenty sided dice.

    roll 1d20

Rolling a three four sided dices plus two.

    roll 3d4+2
